Piera Damonte

      

My career started with fighting diseases at laboratory bench, which led to empowering those living with disease on rivers and mountains, and finally to building online tools that help thousands of scientists fight diseases. In my last position as Operations Director, I borrowed from the analytical and scientific methods I learned as a research scientist and blended them with leadership skills from expeditions to create essential resources for users and drive digital media ROI for customers.

Before science and the outdoors, my very first love was computers. At age 10, I started working odd jobs in order to save up for an Apple computer. To fast forward over learning BASIC, Pascal, and tinkering with HTML and PHP websites, I recently completed the Hackbright Academy Software Engineering Fellowship to gain the technical know-how that I always wanted. In my next career chapters, I want to expand and utilise these skills, and seek opportunities to work with customers and lead teams once again.

The common threads of all of my pursuits include tenacity, compassion, curiosity, and the ever useful sense of humor. I bring to my work a passion for quality, efficiency, and process. Literally translated, my name means "rock of the mountain". While I endeavor to be as solid as my namesake implies, you won't catch me taking myself too seriously.
